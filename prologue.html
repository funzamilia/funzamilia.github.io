Inferus Germen

Prologue

The year is 450 after the discovery of the Inferus Germen solar system. There are now large settlements on all 10 life supporting planets. The two largest planetary systems are the Planetary Republic of Badenia and its neighbour, the Independent Collective of Bavium.

Every year on a day called proxy the two systems are so close to each other that the planet and moons are clearly visible in the sky of the neighbouring planet.
